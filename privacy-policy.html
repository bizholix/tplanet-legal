Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide during registration (email, display name), profile data (avatar, bio, location), listing content, trade history, messages, and ratings.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to operate the platform, facilitate trades, display profiles and ratings, send notifications, and improve the user experience.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your public profile, listings, and ratings are visible to other users. We may share data when required by law.

Communications & Administrative Access

Messages exchanged through TPLANET are stored on our servers so conversations can be delivered, displayed, and recovered for you.

Authorized administrators may access conversations when necessary for moderation, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, security investigations, or enforcement of platform policies.

This access is restricted to authorized personnel only and is never granted to other users.

Every administrative access to a conversation is logged for accountability and security purposes.

We do not routinely monitor private conversations without a valid moderation, safety, or legal reason.

When an administrator reviews a conversation for moderation, the transcript may be processed by an AI assistant to produce a neutral summary and flag potential risks. This processing is limited to moderation purposes, content is not used to train AI models, transcripts are not retained by the AI provider beyond the request, and every AI-assisted review is logged.

AI-assisted reviews are rate-limited per administrator to prevent bulk or automated processing of user conversations.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your personal data through your account settings. You may request account deletion by contacting support.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please reach out through the platform's support channels.
