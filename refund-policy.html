Overview

  • This Refund Policy applies to TPlanet Premium subscriptions.
  • TPlanet offers digital subscription services that provide additional platform features and benefits.
  • By purchasing a Premium subscription, you agree to this Refund Policy.

Refund Eligibility

You may request a refund within 14 days of your initial subscription purchase if all of the following apply:

  • The request is submitted within 14 calendar days of purchase.
  • The subscription was purchased directly through TPlanet.
  • Premium features have not been substantially used.

Refund requests are reviewed individually and may be approved or denied based on account activity and feature usage.

Subscription Renewals

  • Subscription renewals are generally non-refundable once processed.
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time.
  • Cancellation prevents future charges.
  • Premium access remains active until the end of the current billing period.

Technical Issues

  • If Premium features are unavailable due to a verified technical issue caused by TPlanet, you may contact support.
  • TPlanet may provide a refund, account credit, or subscription extension when appropriate.

Abuse Prevention

Refund requests may be denied if:

  • Premium features have been substantially used.
  • The request is fraudulent or abusive.
  • The user violated the Terms of Service.
  • The user engaged in chargeback abuse.

TPlanet reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts involved in fraudulent refund activity.

How To Request A Refund

Email support@tplanet.app from your account email and include:

  • The email address associated with your account.
  • Purchase date.
  • Transaction ID (if available).
  • Reason for the refund request.
  • Any additional details that may help us review your request.

Requests are typically reviewed within 5–10 business days.

Nothing in this Refund Policy limits any rights available under applicable consumer protection laws. Users may have additional rights depending on their jurisdiction.
